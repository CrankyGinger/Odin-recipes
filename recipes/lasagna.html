Lasagna

Description

Lasagna, a classic Italian dish, is a hearty and flavorful layered pasta casserole that tantalizes the taste buds with its rich combination of pasta, cheese, meat, and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Boil Noodles
  2. Prepare Meat Sauce
  3. Mix Ricotta Filling
  4. Assemble the Layers
  5. Bake
  6. Serve
